# SIGNIFICANT CHANGES

Berdasarkan hasil telaah strategis atas bisnis MPMParts dan dengan mempertimbangkan business rational yang sejalan dengan fokus Perservan untuk memperkuat bisnis usaha secara organik, terhitung sejak tanggal 31 Desember 2020 kegiatan operasional MPMParts dihentikan.

Pursuant to the results of strategic review over MPMParts business and with consideration to rational business that is in line with the Company's focus to build a stronger business organically, then as of December 31*, 2020, MPMParts discontinued its operation.
