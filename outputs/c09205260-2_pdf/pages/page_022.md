# Notebooks

The HP EliteBook X G1A 14" features covers that contain at least 80% recycled aluminum,¹⁶ with at least 30% ocean-bound plastic in the speaker box.¹⁶ Additionally, the device's heat plate includes at least 50% recycled copper,¹⁷ and the keycaps are made with at least 50% recycled plastic.¹⁷ This device is also registered EPEAT® Climate+™ Gold in the US.¹⁸

# Desktops

HP OmniDesk Desktop AI PC has 20% recycled steel, 85% recycled ITE waste in bezel, 30% ocean-bound plastic in speaker enclosure, and 25% ocean-bound plastic in internal power supply enclosures. We also included 75% postconsumer recycled plastic in keyboard and mouse enclosures.¹⁷

# Displays

HP Display Series 7 Pro · 734pm &amp; HP 727pm is designed with 90% recycled aluminum and 15% recycled steel in product VESA bracket, column, swivel, base, and chassis bracket.¹⁹

![img-53.jpeg](img-53.jpeg)

# HP Certified Refurbished PCs

A refurbished EliteBook 840 G7, originally registered ENERGY STAR® v8 and made with more than 30% recycled plastic,²⁰ is estimated to have a 72% lower carbon footprint compared with a new equivalent PC.²¹ HP Certified Refurbished PCs are thoroughly refurbished with HP genuine parts and backed with a one-year warranty²² from HP Support Services. The offering is active in France and the US since 2024 and is expanding to more countries in 2025.

# Peripherals

HP 720/725 Multi-Device Rechargeable Wireless Keyboard and Mouse Combo contains at least 60% postconsumer recycled plastic and comes in FSC® Certified packaging.²³ It features a supercapacitor, enhancing durability with longer charge cycles and minimizing reliance on disposable power sources.
