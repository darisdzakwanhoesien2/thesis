# Operating Responsibly

Integrity is at the heart of HP's culture. HP expects to meet the highest ethical standards and treats others with respect and fairness. A strong dedication to our values underpins our efforts, is reinforced by in-depth training and communication, and is upheld through targeted policies and strong governance.

suppliers and partners as we harness our scale and influence to drive progress across the IT industry.
