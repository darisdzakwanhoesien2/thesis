# Employee Engagement

Employee feedback is a critical component of our journey to create a Future Ready HP. We regularly collect feedback from employees to better understand and improve their experiences, and to identify opportunities to strengthen our culture.

Employees can share feedback in a variety of ways, including through our annual Voice Insight Action (VIA) survey and periodic Quick Clicks pulse surveys. In 2024, 90% of employees participated in our annual VIA employee engagement survey.

HP has sustained action to digitize and automate the tools and processes that improve day-to-day experiences for our employees. Overall, 82% of survey participants had a favorable view of the employee experience as measured by our engagement index in 2024. HP continues to be certified as a Great Place to Work, with our employee rating of 86% favorable in 2024. See Recognition.
